Strong Coffee, Strong Connections About Our Cafe

Founded in 2006 by the Hansson brothers, our establishment has been serving up rich coffee sourced from farmers in various regions of South and Central America. We are dedicated to travelling the world, finding the best coffee, and bringing back to you here in our cafe.

We guarantee that you will enjoy our decadent blends the moment you walk inside until you finish your last sip. Join our family for your daily routine, an outing with friends, or simply just to enjoy some alone time.

Co-founder Eric Hansson

Eric Hansson is one of the co-founders of St.Charles Coffee House. Together with his brothers, they started the coffee shop with just a pure passion for coffee and family. At the coffee shop, Eric takes time to get to know the customers, and listens to what they like about the place and what the shop can improve on.

Eric Hansson - "It is very important to me that our customers feels like they are a part of our family when they walk in. Therfore, I want to hear their feedback and get to know them as people. We all are connected here by the amazing cup of coffee people all drink."
